The Digital Fiber Deployment Lifecycle

A Governance & Execution Blueprint

This document outlines the standardized, end-to-end digital workflow for Airtel's fiber optic network deployment, ensuring quality, transparency, and real-time governance from initiation to final handover.

100% Digital Audit Trail
Real-Time Governance
Quality-First Approach

PHASE 1: PLANNING & INITIATION

Step 01

Job Creation & Scope Definition

Objective:

To establish a centralized, unique, and auditable digital record for each project, integrating scope, financials, and vendor details from inception.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • System-generated, unique **Job ID** creation.
  • Integration of Purchase Order (PO) value and terms.
  • Allocation to an approved execution vendor.
Critical Outputs:

Master Project Record (MPR) in the central system.

Step 02

Pre-Construction Survey & Risk Analysis

Objective:

To conduct proactive risk mitigation through detailed on-ground analysis, ensuring all logistical, environmental, and legal requirements are identified and planned for.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Geospatial route mapping and soil condition analysis.
  • Identification of all RoW, utility, and permit requirements (NHAI, etc.).
  • Logging of potential obstacles and creation of a mitigation plan.
Critical Outputs:

Approved Route Plan & Permit Application Dossier.

PHASE 2: EXECUTION & VALIDATION

Step 03

Live Field Execution

Objective:

To enforce standardized, transparent, and accountable fieldwork through mandatory digital documentation for every on-site activity.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Submission of **geo-tagged, time-stamped photo evidence**.
  • Completion of activity-specific digital checklists.
  • Real-time, span-wise progress updates from the field app.
Critical Outputs:

Validated Digital Photo Cards & Completed Checklists.

Step 04

Network Quality Testing

Objective:

To technically validate the performance and integrity of the newly laid fiber network against stringent quality benchmarks.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Per-core **OTDR sweeps** to measure optical loss and reflectance.
  • Splice and connector loss analysis.
  • Digital upload of all trace reports to the Job ID.
Critical Outputs:

Complete Set of OTDR Reports & Fiber Quality Certificate.

Step 05

Multi-Tier Quality Approval

Objective:

To enforce rigorous, multi-level quality assurance through a structured, auditable review process for all submitted field data.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Zonal Quality Head (ZQH) review of all evidence.
  • Logging of **Non-Conformities (NCs)** for any defects found.
  • **Bi-directional workflow** allows tasks to be sent back for rework.
Critical Outputs:

Approved Work Packages & Documented NC Register.

PHASE 3: CLOSURE & GOVERNANCE

Step 06

Acceptance Test Certificate (ATC)

Objective:

To formally close the project execution phase upon successful validation of all work, providing the official sign-off required for financial settlement.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Vendor submission of a formal AT Offer post-approval.
  • Final quality audit by the Circle/National Head.
  • Issuance of the digital **Acceptance Test Certificate (ATC)**.
Critical Outputs:

Digitally Signed & Archived ATC.

Step 07

Milestone-Based Payments

Objective:

To ensure a transparent, automated, and timely financial settlement process linked directly to verified project milestones.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Automated invoice generation triggered by ATC issuance.
  • Payment release as per PO milestones.
  • Management of retention amount for the defect liability period.
Critical Outputs:

Released Payments & Updated Retention Ledger.

Step 08

Live Governance & Reporting

Objective:

To provide management with continuous, data-driven oversight of all projects, enabling proactive decision-making and performance monitoring.

Key Processes & Actions:
  • Real-time performance dashboards (Circle, National level).
  • Complete, immutable digital **audit trail** for every action.
  • Monitoring of risk registers and project health metrics.
Critical Outputs:

Live Dashboards & Comprehensive Project Reports.
